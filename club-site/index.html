About Us

The Long Way Run Club was founded on the belief that consistency matters more than shortcuts. What started as a small group meeting for early miles has grown into a steady weekly rhythm built around showing up, running with purpose, and choosing the longer path when it counts. We’re not here for records or noise. Just disciplined runs, familiar routes, and a community that values the process as much as the finish.
